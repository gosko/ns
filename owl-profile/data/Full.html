Unique identifier for OWL 2 Full.

'http://www.w3.org/ns/owl-profile/Full' is the URI. OWL Full is defined by the OWL 2 Structural Specification and Functional-Style Syntax and the OWL 2 Web Ontology Language, RDF-Based Semantics documents, part of the the OWL 2 W3C Recommendation.

For more information about OWL, please refer to the the OWL 2 Document Overview Recommendation.
