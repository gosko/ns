Unique URIs for OWL 2 Profiles

This is a unique, top-level Class URI for various OWL 2 Profiles and instances.. For the specific entailment regimes, use these URI-s:

  • Full: (http://www.w3.org/ns/owl-profile/Full)
  • DL: (http://www.w3.org/ns/owl-profile/DL)
  • EL: (http://www.w3.org/ns/owl-profile/EL)
  • QL: (http://www.w3.org/ns/owl-profile/QL)
  • RL: (http://www.w3.org/ns/owl-profile/RL)

Note that, strictly speaking, OWL Full and OWL DL are not referred to, formally, as "profiles" by the OWL 2 Profiles specification. However, "each profile is defined as a syntactic restriction of the OWL 2 Structural Specification, i.e., as a subset of the structural elements that can be used in a conforming ontology,â€¦" (see the OWL 2 Document Overview) In this sense, and for practical purposes, including URI-s for OWL Full and OWL DL is correct: OWL Full does not impose any restriction, whereas OWL DL can be seen as a sytactic restriction of OWL Full that is particularly well suited for OWL 2 Direct Semantics. Note also that there are no separate URI-s for the 2004 version of OWL Full and OWL DL; OWL 2 Full and OWL 2 DL, published in 2008 supersede that version of OWL. Nor is there a URI for OWL Lite.
