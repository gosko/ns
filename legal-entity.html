Legal Entity Vocabulary (obsolete)

Th namespace http://www.w3.org/ns/legal-entity was adopted temporarily in 2012 by the Government Linked Data Working Group but is no longer in use and may be re-used in any way in future.

The temporary usage of the namespace by the GLD was related to the evolution of the European Commission ISA Programme's Core Business Vocabulary into what is now the Registered Organization vocabulary.

The RDF schema for RegOrg is available at http://www.w3.org/ns/regorg.

For more background, see this blog post: Interoperable Governments?.
