Unique identifier for Simple Entailment.

“http://www.w3.org/ns/entailment/Simple” is the URI. The specification for the Simple entailment is part of the RDF Semantics W3C Recommendation.

For more information about RDF, please refer to the the RDF Concepts and Abstract Syntax Recommendation .
