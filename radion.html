RADion Vocabulary Holding Page

This is a holding page for the RADion namespace document.

RADion is due to become a work item for the Government Linked Data WG in the very near future at which point we expect to publish the namspace document in various formats at this URI (http://www.w3.org/ns/radion#).

ADMS has been developed under the ISA Programme of the European Union and all documentation, including the current version of the namespace document, is available on the European Commission's Joinup Platform.

For background, see this blog post: Interoperable Governments?.
