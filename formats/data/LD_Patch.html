Unique identifier for the LD Patch Format.

'http://www.w3.org/ns/formats/LD_Patch' is the URI. LD Patch is defined by the “Linked Data Patch Format” W3C Candidate Recommendation. It has a media type: ‘text/ldpatch’ and a preferred suffix: ‘.ldp’. (See the for further details on the Media Registration.)
