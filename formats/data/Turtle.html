Unique identifier for the Turtle file format.

'http://www.w3.org/ns/formats/Turtle' is the URI. Turtle is defined by the “Turtle - Terse RDF Triple Language” W3C Team Submission. It has a media type: ‘text/turtle’ and a preferred suffix: ‘.ttl’. (See further details on the Media Registration.)
