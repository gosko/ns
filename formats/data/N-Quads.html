Unique identifier for the RDF Dataset serialization in N-Quads.

'http://www.w3.org/ns/formats/N-Quads' is the URI. N-Quads is defined the “N-Quads” W3C Recommendation. It uses the general media type: ‘application/n-quads’ and a preferred suffix: ‘.nq’.
