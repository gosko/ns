Unique identifier for the SPARQL Results in CSV file format.

'http://www.w3.org/ns/formats/SPARQL_Results_CSV' is the URI. SPARQL Results in CSV is defined by the “SPARQL 1.1 Query Results CSV and TSV Formats” W3C Recommendation. It has a media type: ‘text/csv’ (though the media type is not SPARQL specific) and a preferred suffix: ‘.csv’. (See further details on the Media Registration.)
