Unique identifier for the RDF serialization in N-Triples.

'http://www.w3.org/ns/formats/N-Triples' is the URI. N-Triples is defined the “N-Triples” W3C Recommendation track document. It uses the general media type: ‘application/n-triples’ and a preferred suffix: ‘.nt’.
